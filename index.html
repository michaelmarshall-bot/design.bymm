    Why 'Product Designer'?

    If you're familiar with the industry you might be wondering why I'm talking about being a 'Product Designer' when I work on layouts and print media too.

    In my experience the modern product design process is an excellent system even outside of digital products - it just needs to be flexible. Advertising goes beyond a single ad campaign, magazines go beyond a single issue, and media goes beyond digital and analogue; your story transcends its format so why shouldn't your process too?
